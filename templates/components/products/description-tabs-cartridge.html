Fast Dispatch
We dispatch 95% of orders the same day when placed before 3pm (Monday to Friday). Orders placed after 3pm, on weekends, or on bank holidays will be shipped the next working day.
Standard Shipping
Most orders are sent via Royal Mail 1st Class or Royal Mail 24hr Tracked. Delivery usually takes 1–3 working days. Please note that delivery times are not guaranteed for untracked services.
Express Courier Option
For faster delivery, you can choose a DPD Express Courier service at checkout. Orders received before 3pm will be dispatched for next working day delivery (UK Mainland only). You’ll receive tracking updates, including delivery time estimates, via email or SMS directly from DPD.
Delivery Areas
We deliver throughout the UK and select European countries. Free UK Mainland delivery on orders over £30 Small surcharges apply for delivery to Northern Ireland, Highlands, Channel Islands, and the following European countries: France, Spain, Germany, Netherlands, Ireland, and others.
Restricted Items
A limited number of products, such as power banks and lithium battery items, may be subject to additional shipping restrictions and delivery charges due to air freight limitations.
